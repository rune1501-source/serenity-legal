Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 6, 2026

1. Overview

Serenity ("we", "us", or "the app") is a breathing and mindfulness application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle data.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Serenity operates entirely on your device. No account creation is required, and no data is transmitted to external servers.

3. Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device:

  • Breathing session history (technique, duration, date)
  • Usage statistics (total sessions, streaks)
  • App preferences (haptic feedback, reminder settings)

This data is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework and is not accessible by us or any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat processes anonymous transaction data (purchase tokens, subscription status) to validate and manage your subscription. RevenueCat does not collect personal information such as your name, email, or Apple ID. For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

All payment processing is handled by Apple through the App Store. We never have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

5. Analytics & Tracking

Serenity does not use any analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, or third-party measurement tools. We do not track your behavior, location, or usage patterns.

6. Advertising

Serenity does not display advertisements and does not share any data with advertising networks.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, lease, or otherwise share any user data with any third party, because we do not collect any data.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the Serenity app will permanently remove all associated data. There is no data on our servers to request deletion of.

9. Children's Privacy

Serenity is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected, no special provisions for children's data are necessary.

10. Security

Since all data remains on your device and is managed by Apple's frameworks, it benefits from the security protections built into iOS, including device encryption and app sandboxing.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
